About Our Business

Max Barnes Electrical prides itself on being Reliable, punctual and friendly electrical service. Max Barnes Electrical offers the best electrical service and quality of work available.If you’re looking for a professional, reliable electricians, Max Barnes Electrical is at your service. Located in Strathalbyn , Max Barnes licensed and expert electricians has decades of experience working with all kinds of homes and businesses to bring quality installation and repair work to all.

As full-time electrical contractors, Strathalbyn and its surroundings have been our home and community for over years, and during that time we’ve provided a full range of installation, maintenance and repair services to our customers in both the city and its suburbs. During that time, we’ve built up a strong base of satisfied customers, and a network of long-lasting professional relationships with people and businesses around the city.

We specialise in providing quality service to set up and fix your Domestic, Industrial , Commerical and Solar Contracting. We put extra care into all our projects, so we can get the job done right the first time, and save you plenty of time, money and peace of mind in the long term.
